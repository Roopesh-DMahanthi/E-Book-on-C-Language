Computer graphics is one of the most interesting and useful topics of computers and c language.Everywhere information is conveyed in the form of pictures,which needs computer graphics understanding.

The header file graphics.h is to be included at the beginning of the program. The header file comprises the definition and explanations of all the graphic functions.
To switch over to graphics mode, a function initgraph() must be invoked.It selects the best resolution and puts the corresponding number in graphics mode.

Few graphics functions :

- initgraph()
- closegraph()
- restorecrtmode()
- graphresult()
- getmaxx() and getmaxy
- line()
- circle()
- arc()
- ellipse()
- bar()
- setcolor()
- outtextxy()
- settextstyle()
- settextjustify()
- getcolor()
